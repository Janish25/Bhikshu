About

With the goal of selling the highest quality marbles, granites, and other natural stones, an experienced team consisting of family members founded Bhikshu Marble and Granite in 1996. Up until 2005, BMG exclusively dealt in native granites and marbles.

Under the name Bhikshu Imports and Exports, it launched a new company that would focus only on importing granite and marble.Bhikshu Groups has satisfied all of its clients, including private and public sectors, businesses, educational institutions, and the hospitality industry.
